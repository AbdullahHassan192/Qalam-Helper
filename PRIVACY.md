# Privacy Policy for Qalam Helper

**Last Updated: January 4, 2026**

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any data.**

This extension:
- Does not collect any personal information
- Does not track your browsing history
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not use analytics or tracking tools

## How It Works

Qalam Helper operates entirely within your browser:
- Reads course information from qalam.nust.edu.pk pages you visit
- Performs calculations locally in your browser
- All data processing happens on your device
- No data ever leaves your browser

## Permissions

The extension only requires permission to run on `https://qalam.nust.edu.pk/*`

This means:
- It only works on NUST Qalam pages
- It cannot access any other websites
- It cannot access data from other tabs or windows

## Data Storage

Any data used by the extension is:
- Temporarily stored in your browser's memory while you use Qalam
- Automatically cleared when you close the page
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Never shared with third parties

## Third-Party Services

This extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/AbdullahHassan192/qalam-helper).

## Consent

By using Qalam Helper, you consent to this privacy policy.
